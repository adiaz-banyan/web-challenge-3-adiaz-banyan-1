Meat-dish

Its a meat dish with vegies and more stuff.

will show you how to create this meal.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Potato
  • Vegies
  1. Take stuff out of packages and wrappers.
  2. Place all stuff in one bowl
  3. Pour 1L 90 proof vodka
  4. Light on fire and wait for fire to go out.
  5. When the fire is out you are ready to eat.
